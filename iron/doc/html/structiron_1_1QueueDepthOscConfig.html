double iron::QueueDepthOscConfig::max_considered_period_secs

We won't use a period of longer than this for the purpose of smoothing. If the highest-energy period is longer than this, then we'll use the highest-energy period that's shorter than this unless it's completely dwarfed by higher periods in terms of energy.

bool iron::QueueDepthOscConfig::use_soft_reset

True if we want soft resets, which means reset the period computations but continue to use the last computed period of smoothing. A hard reset, on the other hand, also stops smoothing until we have a new period.
