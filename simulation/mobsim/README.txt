The Multicast Over Backpressure Simulator (mobsim) requires the following
utility files:

- getNextLine.m
- mskscoptAdapter.m
- processImpairFile.m
- processNonNS3TopoFile.m
- processNS3TopoFile.m
- processTopoFile.m
- processTraffFile.m
- updateVirTopology.m

The above utility files are located in the IRON/analysis/matlab/Utilities
directory.
